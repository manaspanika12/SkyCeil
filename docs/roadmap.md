# Roadmap

## Provider depth

- ADS-B Exchange or FlightAware enrichment for airline, aircraft type, origin, and destination.
- Local ADS-B receiver ingestion through readsb or dump1090.
- Provider health metrics and fallback priority rules.

## Projection and installation

- Multi-room profiles.
- Guided calibration with camera-assisted corner detection.
- Kiosk packaging for Windows and mini-PC deployments.

## Visualization modules

- ISS and satellite layers.
- Weather radar and cloud-top overlays.
- Constellation and celestial coordinate modules.
- Time-lapse and cinematic recording modes.

## Operations

- Admin settings UI.
- Structured logs and metrics dashboard.
- Crash reporting.
- Secure remote update channel for commercial installations.
